Avatar

In Japanese raccoon lore, leaves symbolize power and magic. Raccoons get power from leaves to transform their appearance. "Fox's wedding" (The Kitsune no Yomeiri) in Japanese folklore means sunshowers. This phrase means that foxes make rain on sunny days to trick people and keep them away from their weddings. The avatar story below mixes these two Japanese lore. It's about a raccoon trying to transform into a fox bride but failing because its tail doesn't fully change.
